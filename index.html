Welcome to Vijayawada

The Business Capital of Andhra Pradesh

Vijayawada, situated on the banks of the Krishna River, is one of the largest cities in Andhra Pradesh. The city is known for its historical significance, with the famous Kanaka Durga Temple perched atop Indrakeeladri Hill being a major spiritual center. The temple, dedicated to Goddess Durga, attracts thousands of devotees throughout the year and is particularly vibrant during the Dasara festival.

The Krishna River plays a vital role in the city's identity and economy. The three-kilometer-long Prakasam Barrage across the Krishna River serves as both a reservoir and a road bridge, making it an engineering marvel and a popular tourist attraction. The city's strategic location and excellent connectivity have made it a major commercial and agricultural hub, earning it the nickname "The Business Capital of Andhra Pradesh."
